DUSKY LEAF MONKEYS

The dusky leaf monkey (also known as the dusky langur or Trachypithecus obscurus) is a fascinating and adorable primate. Here's an amazing fact that stands out:
They're Born Bright Orange! Dusky leaf monkey babies are born with bright orange or golden fur, which is a huge contrast to the adults' soft gray-black coats.
